# Project overview — Mathematical Modeling of Division Burden

A single theoretical script: a stochastic branching-process simulation of HSC self-renewal,
differentiation and death, comparing simulated division-burden/pool-size trajectories against
closed-form analytical formulas, to provide a mechanistic interpretation of the empirically
observed HB (high-burden) vs LB (low-burden) clone phenotypes used throughout the rest of the
codebase. (A byte-identical copy of this script exists at the Downloads root and in its own
subfolder; both are included.)
