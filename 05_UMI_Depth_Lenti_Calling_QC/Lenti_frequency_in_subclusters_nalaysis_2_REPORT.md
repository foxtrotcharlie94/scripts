# Lenti_frequency_in_subclusters_nalaysis_2.R

**Script:** `Lenti_frequency_in_subclusters_nalaysis_2.R`

## What it does
Same pipeline as Lenti_frequency_in_subclusters_nalaysis.R plus two extra appended sections: formal HB-vs-LB statistical comparison of Lenti+ % (Mann-Whitney/Welch/Student t-tests with bracket-annotated plot), and UMI sequencing-depth distribution plots (violin and median-per-sample) for both cell types.

## Inputs
Same as the sibling file.

## Outputs
All sibling outputs plus lenti_freq_HBvsLB_stats.pdf, lenti_freq_stats.csv, umi_depth_violin_per_sample.pdf, umi_depth_median_per_sample.pdf, umi_depth_combined.pdf.

## Conceptual aim / target
Same as sibling, extended to formally test HB-vs-LB Lenti+ frequency differences and characterize raw sequencing depth per sample/cell type.

## Note
Near-duplicate/extended superset of Lenti_frequency_in_subclusters_nalaysis.R.
